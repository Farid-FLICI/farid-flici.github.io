Mortality Datasets

Mortality Surface, Algeria (1977–2014)

This dataset reconstructs the full mortality surface for Algeria using ONS life tables. Missing years were estimated, and mortality rates were interpolated to single ages and extended to older ages.

Source: ONS publications. Methods detailed in Flici (2015, 2020, 2025).

Fertility Datasets

This dataset reconstructs the fertility surface in Algeria using data from the Office for National Statistics (ONS), complemented by census and survey data. Missing years were estimated, and fertility rates were interpolated to obtain continuous age-specific fertility rates.

Source: ONS publications, censuses, and surveys. Methods detailed in Flici (2016).
