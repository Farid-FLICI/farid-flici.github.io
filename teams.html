Population Studies

The Population Studies Research Team is interested in studying population related phenomena using a mixture of quantitative and qualitative approaches. The team's research agenda focusses on understanding population dynamics in Algeria (e.g.longevity, mortality, fertility, nuptiality, devortiality) and households and communities behaviour regarding finance and consumption.
