JAVA - Journee d'Actuariat-Vie Algerie (2016-2018)

Starting in January 2016, I initiated a series of workshops known as Journée d'Actuariat Vie Algérie - JAVA which is dedicated to Life Insurance, Social Security, and Retirement in Algeria and in the world. Until the end of 2018, 7 editions have be organized with 15 talks and 10 speackers.
