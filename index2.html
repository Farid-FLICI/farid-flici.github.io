About Me

Senior Research Fellow at CREAD (Algeria), Visiting Researcher at LSHTM (UK). PhD in Statistics and Demography (ENSSEA, 2017). I specialize in mortality modeling, migrant health inequalities, population aging, and pension sustainability. My work bridges actuarial science and public policy to address demographic challenges in Algeria and Europe.

Research Interests

  • Migrant mortality and health disparities in high-income countries
  • Population aging and retirement system sustainability
  • Expert-based demographic projections and forecasting tools
  • Chronic diseases and healthy life expectancy
  • Applied statistical modeling (R, Shiny, mortality interpolation)
