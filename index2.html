About

Senior Research Fellow at the Research Center in Applied Economics for Development (CREAD), Algiers. Visiting Researcher at the London School of Hygiene & Tropical Medicine (LSHTM). PhD in Statistics (ENSSEA, 2017). Specialized in mortality modeling, migrant health inequalities, population aging, and pension system sustainability. Research bridges actuarial science, demography, and public policy to address demographic challenges in Algeria and Europe.

Research Interests

  • Migrant mortality and health inequalities in high-income countries
  • Population aging and retirement system sustainability
  • Expert-based demographic projections and forecasting
  • Chronic disease impact on healthy life expectancy
  • Applied statistical modeling (R, Shiny, mortality interpolation)
