About

I am a Senior Research Fellow at CREAD (Algiers) and Visiting Researcher at LSHTM (London), specializing in actuarial demography. My research focuses on mortality modelling, migrant health inequalities in high-income countries, and the sustainability of pension and social protection systems.

I combine demographic methods, actuarial science, and statistical programming (R, Shiny) to develop both academic research and policy-relevant tools. My work contributes to international debates on population ageing, health expectancy, and social security reform, with applications in Algeria and comparative European contexts.
