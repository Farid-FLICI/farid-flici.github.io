About

Senior Research Fellow at CREAD (Algeria) with expertise in demography, actuarial science, and social security. PhD in Statistics (ENSSEA, 2017). Current visiting researcher at LSHTM (UK). Research interests include migrant health disparities, population aging, pension sustainability, and statistical modeling.

Research Interests

  • Migrant mortality and health inequalities in high-income countries
  • Population aging and retirement system sustainability
  • Expert-based demographic projections and population forecasting
  • Chronic disease impact on healthy life expectancy
  • Applied statistical modeling (R, Shiny, mortality interpolation)
