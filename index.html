About

Farid is an Actuary, he holds a PhD in Applied Statistics. He works as a Researcher at the Research Center in Applied Economics for Developement - CREAD in Algiers (Algeria). His research interest focusses on mortality & longevity modeling, ageing, and pensios sustainability. At Cread, he is leading the Population Studies Team and the Actuarial Demography Working Group. Besides, he is a member-expert at the national committee for population belonging to the Algerian health ministry.
