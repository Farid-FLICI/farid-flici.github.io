Overview

HDSE Research Department is one of the four research departments of the Research Center in Applied Economics for Development - CREAD (Algiers, Algeria). HDSE is concerned with studying and analysing social phenomena and their implication on the economy. The HDSE research Agenda is focussed on observing recent trends in human development indicator components and investigating ways for their promotion. With Education, Health, and Labor being the main components of Human Development Indicators, our research departments tries to analyse and explain changes and behaviours.

On the other hand, we are intersted in a wide range of population-related discipline such as demography, household economics and finance, labor economics, social protection, pensions, shadow economy, mobility, migration, health transiton, gender, youth, elderly, ... etc.
